Microphone for Siri

Hold to talk to Siri for voice search, playback control, and quick system commands.

Power

Press and hold to turn your TV or connected equipment on or off.

Touch-enabled clickpad

Swipe to move through menus, then click the center to select an item.

Back

Returns to the previous screen or backs out of the current menu.

TV/Home

Opens the Apple TV Home screen and can switch back to the TV view.

Play/Pause

Starts or pauses playback for the current movie, show, song, or app.

Mute

Instantly silences the audio until you press it again or adjust volume.

Volume

Raises or lowers the sound level on your TV, receiver, or soundbar.
